About us

Cyber Hat Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a pioneering cybersecurity company based in India, driven by the mission to secure the digital world through innovation, education, and trust. We specialize in Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing (VAPT), compliance audits, cyber forensics, and awareness programs for both public and private sectors. Our certified professionals bring extensive hands-on expertise, helping organizations strengthen their defense systems and respond effectively to modern cyber threats. 

Beyond services, Cyber Hat Solutions takes pride in building a cyber-aware society. We’ve collaborated with government agencies, police departments, universities, and corporate organizations to conduct nationwide training programs, hackathons, and workshops — empowering thousands with practical knowledge to fight cybercrime. Our partnerships with reputed organizations like Accenture, Chandigarh University, Quick Heal Academy, and Sree Vidyaniketan Engineering College reflect our commitment to excellence and trust. 

At Cyber Hat, we believe cybersecurity isn’t just about technology — it’s about people, awareness, and resilience. Our goal is to make security simple, accessible, and effective for everyone, ensuring that every digital interaction remains private, protected, and future-ready.
