Cybercrime Awareness & Training for Cyberabad Police

Cyber Hat Empowers Cyberabad Police with Advanced Cybercrime Training

Cyber Hat Solutions organized a specialized Cybercrime Investigation & Digital Forensics Training Program for the Cyberabad Police, aimed at strengthening their capabilities in tackling cybercrimes, social media threats, and online frauds.

More than 90 officers from various departments actively participated in the hands-on sessions. The initiative was highly appreciated by Commissioner V.C. Sajjanar, who also unveiled Cyber Hat’s official logo and website during the event.

This collaboration highlights Cyber Hat Solutions’ commitment to empowering law enforcement with real-world cybersecurity expertise and building a digitally resilient society.
